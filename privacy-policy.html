HydroSleep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

The short version: HydroSleep collects no data. Everything you enter stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no access to your information.

1. Who we are

HydroSleep is a personal Android application developed by Filip Kugler (filipkugler@gmail.com). It is available on the Google Play Store for a one-time purchase of $0.99 with no subscriptions or in-app purchases.

2. Data storage — on your device only

All data you enter in HydroSleep — including sleep records, hydration logs, temperature measurements, medication entries, growth measurements, vaccination records, milestones, and child profiles — is stored exclusively in the local storage (AsyncStorage) of your Android device.

We do not:

  • collect or transmit any personal data to our servers (we have none)
  • require account registration or login
  • use any analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs
  • show advertisements
  • sell or share your data with any third party

3. Optional NAS synchronisation

HydroSleep includes an optional sync feature that allows you to back up your data to a NAS (Network-Attached Storage) device you own and control, via the WebDAV protocol. This feature:

  • is entirely opt-in and requires you to configure your own server address and credentials
  • only communicates with the server address you provide — typically on your home local network
  • never routes data through any third-party server or cloud service operated by us

The NAS credentials you enter are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us.

4. Push notifications

HydroSleep uses Expo's push notification service to send real-time alerts between two family devices (e.g. when a child falls asleep or wakes up). If you enable this feature:

  • A push token is generated by the Expo SDK on your device and stored on your own NAS (not on any server operated by us).
  • Notification payloads contain only brief event labels (e.g. child name + "fell asleep") and carry no health data.
  • Expo's push infrastructure is operated by Expo (expo.dev) under their own privacy policy.

You can disable push notifications at any time in your Android system settings.

5. Photos

If you add a child profile photo, the image is stored solely in the local storage of your device (and optionally synced to your own NAS as described above). No photos are uploaded to any server operated by us.

6. Children's data

HydroSleep is designed to track data about infants and young children. All such data is stored locally on the parent's device and is never accessible to us or any third party.

7. Permissions

The app may request the following Android permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — to send local and push notifications (sleep timers, hydration reminders, partner alerts)
  • INTERNET — solely for NAS synchronisation and Expo push notifications; no other network calls are made
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — on older Android versions, for saving exported PDF reports to your device
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES — for selecting a child profile photo from your gallery

8. Data deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Backup & Restore" section in the app's Settings to clear local data
  • Uninstalling the app (this removes all locally stored data)
  • Manually deleting files from your NAS if you used the sync feature

Because we never receive your data, there is nothing to request deletion of from our side.

9. Third-party services

HydroSleep uses the following third-party SDKs or services, solely as described above:

  • Expo — app framework and push notification delivery

No other analytics, advertising, or data-sharing SDKs are included.

10. Changes to this policy

If the privacy practices of HydroSleep change materially, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date will be revised. Given the nature of the app (no data collection), significant changes are unlikely.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to filipkugler@gmail.com.
